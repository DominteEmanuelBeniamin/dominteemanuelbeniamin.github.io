Players Guide

Welcome to Concord Iași Open, Players!

On behalf of the entire Iași Open team, I am thrilled to welcome you to this year's ATP Challenger 100 tournament! We are honored to have players of your caliber competing in the event and we are eager to witness exciting matches on our courts.

This Player's Guide contains important information about the tournament, including key contacts, accommodation details, special events like Players Night, and a handy guide to the city of Iași. We hope this information will help you feel welcome and comfortable during your stay.

We wish you the best of luck in the tournament and a pleasant stay in Iași!

Sincerely,
Andrei Pavel
Tournament Director, Iași Open

P.S. Please do not hesitate to contact the tournament staff if you have any questions or require assistance. We are here to help you have a memorable experience at Iași Open!
