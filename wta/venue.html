Player Services

Players Desk

The Players Desk will be open daily from 8:00 AM until the conclusion of the last match.

Catering

Meal vouchers can be purchased at the Players Desk for €10 each. These vouchers can be redeemed at designated catering areas for a delicious and convenient meal option.

Gym Facilities

A well-equipped 70sqm training room is located on-site at the tournament venue for your pre-match warm-up or workout needs. In addition, complimentary access is available to a state-of-the-art gym.

Official Ball

Official Ball is Dunlop. Practice balls available at the Players Desk.

Stringing Services

Stringing services are available for a fee. Please contact the Players Desk for current pricing and to schedule your racquet.
